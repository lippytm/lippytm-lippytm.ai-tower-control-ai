# Ecosystem Health Check Model

This document defines a reusable way to review the health of the wider `lippytm` ecosystem as it expands across education, AI systems, cybersecurity, sandboxes, products, memberships, affiliates, and community participation.

## Purpose
Make it easier to see what is healthy, what is drifting, what needs updates, and what should be strengthened, archived, or promoted.

## Core Principle
A growing ecosystem stays viable when its systems are reviewed in small repeatable checks instead of only after major problems appear.

## Health Check Areas

### 1. Structure Health
Review:
- repo role clarity
- link quality between major repos
- duplication vs useful specialization
- documentation coverage

### 2. Learning Health
Review:
- lesson clarity
- curriculum usefulness
- support for mixed environments
- reusable teaching patterns
- builder review loops

### 3. Workflow Health
Review:
- workflow clarity
- secure workflow boundaries
- sandbox vs operational separation
- review checkpoints

### 4. Product Health
Review:
- kit clarity
- supportability
- update cycle visibility
- release pattern consistency

### 5. Distribution Health
Review:
- membership update quality
- affiliate support quality
- content variant clarity
- product variant maintainability

### 6. Community Health
Review:
- contributor pathway clarity
- member pathway clarity
- partner pathway clarity
- collaboration usefulness

## Best Practices
- keep health checks simple enough to repeat
- record weak areas as improvement opportunities
- use health checks to decide what should scale next
- archive stale or confusing systems clearly
- promote strong repeated patterns into templates and kits
