# ManyChat and BotBuilders Intake Workflow

Purpose: create a front-line conversation workflow for Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, ManyChat, and BotBuilders that captures leads and routes them into the lippytm.ai Business-of-Businesses automation network.

Quality is Job #1. Human review required before publishing customer-facing flows.

## Trigger

A person messages, clicks a CTA, responds to a post, fills an intake, or asks about:

- AI automation
- chatbot setup
- business funding
- AI websites
- affiliate tools
- Twin agents
- GitHub/repo upgrades
- support services

## Lead categories

- AI Business Automation lead
- Chatbot/ManyChat/BotBuilders lead
- Business funding lead
- AI website/app lead
- AI coding/repo upgrade lead
- Affiliate opportunity lead
- Support question
- Not ready / nurture lead

## Intake questions

1. What are you trying to build or improve?
2. Are you looking for leads, automation, funding, a website, chatbot support, or repo/app help?
3. What platform are you using now?
4. What is the biggest problem you want solved first?
5. What is your preferred follow-up method?

## Prompt

```text
You are the ManyChat and BotBuilders Intake Flow Designer for lippytm.ai.

Create a short chatbot intake flow that:

1. Welcomes the lead
2. Identifies what they need
3. Classifies the lead category
4. Asks only necessary questions
5. Routes them to the best next action
6. Creates a safe follow-up message
7. Avoids unsupported guarantees
8. Creates a GitHub issue note for workflow improvement if needed

Connect the lead to one of these offers:
- AI Business Automation Starter Kit
- Chatbot Lead Follow-Up Setup
- Funding Intake + Automation Bundle
- AI Website + Chatbot Launch Kit
- AI Coding Repo Upgrade Kit
- Affiliate Automation Engine
- AI Support Desk Starter
```

## Sample welcome

```text
Hi! I help organize AI automation, chatbot follow-up, business funding intake, AI websites, and business systems. What are you trying to improve first — leads, funding, chatbot setup, website, automation, or support?
```

## Sample route

```text
Thanks — based on that, the best next step is to start with [offer/workflow]. I can help organize your information and prepare the next action.
```

## Review boundary

Drafting flows is automation-friendly. Publishing, making funding claims, or sending financial/legal/tax/investment/trading language requires human review.

## Definition of done

- [ ] Lead categories are clear
- [ ] Intake questions are short
- [ ] Routing path is clear
- [ ] Follow-up is safe and realistic
- [ ] No unsupported guarantees are made
- [ ] Support escalation path exists
