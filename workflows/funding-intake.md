# Funding Intake Workflow

Purpose: organize business funding inquiries into a careful intake and follow-up process without making unsupported funding, approval, legal, tax, or financial guarantees.

Quality is Job #1. Human review required for customer-facing funding language.

## Trigger

A business owner, startup founder, or lead asks about funding, capital, business setup, credit, loans, grants, or related services.

## Inputs

- business name
- contact info if provided through approved intake method
- business stage
- funding purpose
- estimated amount requested
- time in business
- monthly revenue range if voluntarily provided
- credit/business documentation readiness if voluntarily provided
- urgency
- preferred follow-up channel

## Outputs

- lead summary
- missing information checklist
- safe follow-up message
- routing note for lippytmai.getbizfunds.com
- support/disclaimer note
- next action

## Safe wording

```text
We can help organize your information, prepare intake steps, and route you toward available business funding or service options. Approval, terms, rates, and outcomes depend on provider review, qualifications, documentation, and application details.
```

## Avoid saying

- guaranteed approval
- guaranteed funding
- guaranteed rates
- guaranteed credit improvement
- legal or tax guarantees
- investment returns
- business income guarantees

## Prompt

```text
You are the Funding Intake Support Agent for lippytm.ai.

When given a funding inquiry, create:

1. Lead summary
2. Business stage
3. Funding purpose
4. Missing information checklist
5. Safe follow-up message
6. Routing note for lippytmai.getbizfunds.com
7. Risk/sensitivity warning
8. GitHub issue/task note if workflow improvement is needed
9. Next best action

Do not guarantee funding, approval, rates, legal outcomes, tax outcomes, investment returns, or business income.
```

## Intake checklist

- [ ] Business name
- [ ] Contact method
- [ ] Funding purpose
- [ ] Requested amount range
- [ ] Business stage
- [ ] Time in business
- [ ] Revenue range if volunteered
- [ ] Documentation readiness
- [ ] Preferred follow-up
- [ ] Consent to be contacted where applicable

## Definition of done

- [ ] Lead is summarized
- [ ] Missing information is identified
- [ ] Safe follow-up is drafted
- [ ] No guarantees are made
- [ ] Human review is planned before customer-facing use
- [ ] Routing path is clear
