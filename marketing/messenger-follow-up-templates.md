# Messenger Follow-Up Templates

Reusable Messenger, Facebook, Instagram, ManyChat, and BotBuilders follow-up messages for lippytm.ai offers.

Quality is Job #1. Review before publishing or sending. Avoid unsupported income, funding, legal, tax, investment, trading, or medical claims.

---

```text
Thanks for reaching out! I help organize AI automation systems for lead follow-up, chatbot workflows, support, websites, affiliate promotion, and business operations.

What are you trying to improve first — leads, follow-up, funding intake, website, chatbot, support, or automation?
```
