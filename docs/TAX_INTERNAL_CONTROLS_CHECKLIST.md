# Tax Internal Controls Checklist

## Purpose
This checklist is a practical internal-control tool for improving accounting accountability, tax-return support readiness, audit preparation, and review discipline across the Business of Businesses ecosystem.

It is meant for internal use and professional collaboration support. It does not replace qualified accounting or tax advice.

## How to Use
- review monthly, quarterly, and year-end
- mark each control as complete, incomplete, not applicable, or needs escalation
- attach document references where relevant
- note owner, reviewer, and follow-up date for unresolved items

## Status Key
- `complete`
- `incomplete`
- `not_applicable`
- `needs_escalation`

## Suggested Best Practices
- standardize reference IDs for documents and schedules
- assign an owner to every unresolved exception
- separate bookkeeping cleanup issues from true tax-judgment issues
- review monthly to reduce year-end bottlenecks
- use dashboards or bots only as support tools under human review
- preserve a clear audit trail from transaction to report section
