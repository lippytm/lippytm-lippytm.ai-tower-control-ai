# Partner Activation Missions

This document explains how partner-origin activation and onboarding signals should become missions in the Control Tower.

## Mission Triggers
- partner-origin activation stalls
- service handoff quality is weak
- premium activation timing is poor
- post-activation follow-up is unclear

## Mission Types
- partner activation mission
- service handoff mission
- premium timing mission
- post-activation follow-up mission

## Best Practices
- keep activation context preserved from partner source to service state
- prioritize activation friction that directly affects money flow
- use post-activation signals to improve partner paths
- keep partner activation changes bounded and measurable
