# Revenue-Priority Missions

This document explains how the Control Tower should create missions from revenue, CRM, and activation signals.

## Mission Triggers
- qualified leads are not converting
- premium paths are underperforming
- checkout or activation friction appears
- lead routing quality is weak

## Mission Types
- revenue clarity mission
- lead-routing mission
- premium activation mission
- checkout handoff mission

## Best Practices
- prioritize money-adjacent friction first
- protect high-impact public surfaces
- keep commerce handling modular
- turn repeated revenue issues into template, workflow, and policy upgrades
