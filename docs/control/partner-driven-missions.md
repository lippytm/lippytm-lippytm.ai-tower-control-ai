# Partner-Driven Missions

This document explains how partner and affiliate signals should become missions in the Control Tower.

## Mission Triggers
- partner inquiry volume increases
- partner-origin leads show strong fit
- partner follow-up quality is weak
- partner activation paths need review

## Mission Types
- partner routing mission
- affiliate clarity mission
- partner follow-up mission
- partner activation mission

## Best Practices
- keep partner missions separate from standard customer routing when useful
- use partner-origin outcomes as quality signals
- improve clear partner paths before scaling promotion volume
- turn repeated partner issues into workflow and template upgrades
