# Customer-Expansion Missions

This document explains how customer expansion and repeat-engagement signals should become missions in the Control Tower.

## Mission Triggers
- active customers show expansion fit
- repeat engagement increases around a related offer
- follow-up timing appears weak
- upsell or cross-sell clarity is low

## Mission Types
- customer expansion routing mission
- repeat engagement mission
- follow-up timing mission
- expansion clarity mission

## Best Practices
- use real service context for expansion decisions
- keep missions tied to useful next steps
- avoid generic upsell pressure
- improve expansion paths through measured outcomes
