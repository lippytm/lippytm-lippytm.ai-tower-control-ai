# Priority-Driven Mission Order

This document explains how the Control Tower should order improvement missions across the portfolio.

## Goal

Sequence missions so the ecosystem improves the most important areas first while preserving long-term innovation.

## Mission Order

1. critical control, hub, and commerce missions
2. high-risk public revenue and product missions
3. routing, replay, and swarm recovery missions
4. monetization and activation improvement missions
5. knowledge and trust-building missions
6. lab exploration and promotion missions

## Best Practices

- combine risk, money flow, and public-surface impact
- keep missions bounded and lane-aware
- do not let lower-risk experiments displace urgent business work
- convert repeated urgent missions into better templates and policies
