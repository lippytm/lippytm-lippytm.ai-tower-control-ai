# CRM-Driven Missions

This document explains how CRM signals should generate improvement and service-delivery missions in the Control Tower.

## Goal

Use CRM events and contact state to create better-timed missions for routing, premium follow-up, activation, and retention.

## Common Mission Triggers
- repeated qualified leads with weak conversion
- premium leads needing higher-touch routing
- service activation bottlenecks
- follow-up backlog growth
- reengagement opportunities

## Mission Types
- lead-routing mission
- premium activation mission
- follow-up quality mission
- service handoff mission
- retention or reengagement mission

## Best Practices
- tie missions to clear CRM events
- keep public-surface changes bounded and reviewable
- use CRM signals to improve timing, clarity, and activation quality
- turn repeated CRM bottlenecks into template and workflow upgrades
