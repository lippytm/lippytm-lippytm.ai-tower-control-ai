# Affiliate Revenue Missions

This document explains how affiliate and partner-origin revenue signals should become missions in the Control Tower.

## Mission Triggers
- partner-origin lead quality is weak
- affiliate conversion quality drops
- premium routing from affiliate traffic underperforms
- partner follow-up quality is inconsistent

## Mission Types
- affiliate clarity mission
- partner-origin routing mission
- premium affiliate path mission
- affiliate follow-up mission

## Best Practices
- measure affiliate quality, not only traffic volume
- protect premium and activation paths before scaling promotions
- use partner-origin signals to improve routing and clarity
- turn repeated affiliate issues into workflow and template upgrades
