# Project to Platform Matrix

## Purpose
This document maps projects and project types to best-fit platform surfaces across the lippytm ecosystem.

## Project Types
- architecture and governance
- product development
- service packaging
- application development
- educational system development
- media and narrative development
- CRM and growth workflows
- reporting and accountability systems
- AI systems and solutions

## Platforms
- GitHub
- MyClaw
- ChatGPT / OpenAI
- Claude
- Grok
- ManyChat
- BotBuilders
- websites / dashboards

## Matrix
| Project Type | GitHub | MyClaw | ChatGPT / OpenAI | Claude | Grok | ManyChat | BotBuilders | Websites / Dashboards | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| architecture and governance | strong | low | support | support | support | low | low | support | GitHub remains canonical |
| product development | strong | support | support | support | support | support | strong | strong | packaging varies by product |
| service packaging | support | strong | support | support | support | support | strong | strong | often multi-surface |
| application development | strong | support | support | support | support | support | support | strong | depends on audience |
| educational system development | strong | support | strong | strong | support | low | support | strong | reusable across many surfaces |
| media and narrative development | support | low | strong | strong | support | support | support | strong | cross-format packaging opportunity |
| CRM and growth workflows | support | strong | support | low | low | strong | support | support | MyClaw/ManyChat strongest operational surfaces |
| reporting and accountability systems | strong | support | strong | strong | support | low | low | strong | governance and traceability remain important |
| AI systems and solutions | strong | support | strong | strong | support | support | support | strong | packaging and delivery vary by solution |

## Best Practices
- use this matrix to guide delivery choices early
- keep GitHub as the authoritative architecture and tracker layer
- allow multi-platform delivery when packaging and operations differ
- keep review level visible when one project spans several platforms
