# AI Development Systems and Solutions Model

## Purpose
This document defines a model for manufacturing AI development systems and solutions across the lippytm ecosystem.

The goal is to connect repositories, products, services, applications, educational systems, CRM systems, media systems, and reporting systems into a repeatable AI-development production framework.

## Core Vision
Treat AI development as a layered manufacturing system that can produce:
- AI-enabled products
- AI-enabled services
- AI-enabled applications
- internal AI development utilities
- educational AI-development modules
- CRM and growth-support AI workflows
- reporting and documentation AI tools

## Core Layers
### Layer 1 — Asset Layer
Repo assets, components, concepts, prompts, templates, data structures, and workflows.

### Layer 2 — Solution Layer
AI-enabled solutions such as automations, copilots, assistants, dashboards, learning systems, or campaign systems.

### Layer 3 — Delivery Layer
GitHub, websites, dashboards, CRM systems, chat surfaces, media surfaces, and platform adapters.

### Layer 4 — Governance Layer
Review levels, documentation, traceability, transparency, diagnostics, and scorecards.

### Layer 5 — Growth Layer
Reuse, packaging, promotion, and portfolio expansion.

## Swarm Roles
### AI Solution Discovery Swarm
Finds where AI development can improve repo, product, or service value.

### AI Solution Structuring Swarm
Defines solution boundaries, object mappings, and delivery surfaces.

### AI Solution Packaging Swarm
Packages AI systems into products, services, applications, or internal assets.

### AI Solution Governance Swarm
Maintains review discipline, transparency, and documentation quality.

### AI Solution Growth Swarm
Expands strong solutions across more repos, products, services, and domains.

## Suggested Solution Categories
- AI development utilities
- AI documentation systems
- AI reasoning and diagnostics systems
- AI CRM and growth systems
- AI reporting and accountability systems
- AI learning and teaching systems
- AI narrative and media systems
- AI interoperability and sync systems

## Best Practices
- define the solution boundary before scaling it
- keep reusable core assets separate from delivery packaging
- connect each AI solution to stable IDs, catalogs, and trackers
- let governance and review travel with the solution as it expands
- prioritize solutions that strengthen more than one category when appropriate
