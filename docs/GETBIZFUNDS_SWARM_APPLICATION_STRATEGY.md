# lippytmai.getbizfunds.com Swarm Application Strategy

## Purpose
This document defines a swarm application strategy for `lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-` as a business-growth, CRM, educational-business, and media-support surface within the broader lippytm ecosystem.

## Strategic Role
`lippytmai.getbizfunds.com-` can function as a high-priority business application surface for:
- startup and business offer presentation
- CRM intake and follow-up coordination
- educational-business content packaging
- ad-copy and campaign support
- funnel and conversion support systems
- business-of-businesses positioning and narrative continuity

## Core Swarm Classes for This Scope
### 1. Offer Structuring Swarm
Supports:
- offer architecture
- package comparison
- business value framing
- startup positioning

### 2. CRM Intake and Follow-Up Swarm
Supports:
- lead intake
- follow-up logic
- status routing
- missing information requests
- nurture workflows

### 3. Educational Business Content Swarm
Supports:
- explainer content
- learning-based offer pages
- knowledge-to-offer packaging
- educational-business trust-building assets

### 4. Ad Copy and Campaign Swarm
Supports:
- headline variants
- campaign narrative structures
- promotional drafts
- funnel copy alignment

### 5. Revenue Traceability Swarm
Supports:
- CRM-to-revenue linkage
- funnel performance notes
- campaign-source traceability
- business-unit mapping

### 6. Governance and Review Swarm
Supports:
- customer-facing review boundaries
- business-claim review support
- legal/policy/tax-sensitive escalation boundaries where relevant

## Best Practices

- align CRM workflows with offer and reporting structures
- keep customer-facing claims under review controls
- reuse strong campaign concepts across multiple channels
- connect growth surfaces back to entity IDs, cost centers, and traceability systems
