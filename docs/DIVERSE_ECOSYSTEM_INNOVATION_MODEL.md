# Diverse Ecosystem Innovation Model

## Purpose
This document defines an innovation model for applying Super Artificial DevOps Synthetic Intelligence Agents and Swarms across multiple ecosystem categories within the lippytm portfolio.

## Core Goal
Support innovation across different operating areas while keeping reuse, governance, and manufacturing structure clear.

## Ecosystem Categories
- repositories and software systems
- products and services
- educational and learning systems
- CRM and growth systems
- reporting and accountability systems
- media and narrative systems
- synchronization systems
- reasoning and documentation systems

## Core Innovation Dimensions
### Domain Coverage
An innovation may support more than one operating category.

### Delivery Coverage
An innovation may be delivered through more than one platform.

### Output Coverage
A concept may become a product, service, application, learning module, media asset, or factory asset.

### Review Coverage
Different output paths may require different review levels.

## Suggested Swarm Roles
### Cross-Domain Mapping Swarm
Maps where one idea or asset can support multiple categories.

### Reuse Promotion Swarm
Turns broad-fit ideas into reusable assets.

### Review Alignment Swarm
Keeps review levels and documentation aligned as innovations expand.

## Best Practices
- prefer innovations that improve more than one category
- map cross-category reuse explicitly
- separate reusable core assets from category-specific packaging
- keep review levels visible when one idea is used in multiple places
- update registers and trackers when an asset expands into more categories
