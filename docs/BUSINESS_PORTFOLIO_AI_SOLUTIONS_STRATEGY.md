# Business Portfolio AI Solutions Strategy

## Purpose
This document defines a strategy for applying AI systems and solutions across the lippytm Business of Businesses portfolio.

The goal is to turn portfolio assets into coordinated AI-enabled growth surfaces for products, services, applications, educational systems, media systems, CRM systems, and reporting systems.

## Strategic Goal
Support a portfolio where AI solutions can:
- increase reuse across repositories
- improve product and service clarity
- strengthen business-growth applications
- improve CRM and campaign coordination
- improve documentation and transparency
- support learning, teaching, and media packaging

## Strategy Layers
### Layer 1 — Portfolio Opportunity Layer
Identify high-value surfaces where AI systems can improve output quality, speed, reuse, or growth.

### Layer 2 — Solution Layer
Define the AI-enabled product, service, application, or support system.

### Layer 3 — Packaging Layer
Turn the solution into an offer, application profile, service package, educational module, or internal utility.

### Layer 4 — Delivery Layer
Connect the solution to GitHub, websites, CRM systems, dashboards, chat surfaces, or campaign/media surfaces.

### Layer 5 — Measurement Layer
Track maturity, reuse, value, documentation quality, and cross-domain expansion.

## Suggested Strategic Focus Areas
- repo-to-product acceleration
- service packaging acceleration
- CRM and follow-up automation support
- portfolio documentation and transparency support
- educational-business asset creation
- media and narrative packaging support
- accountability and reporting support
- interoperability and synchronization support

## Best Practices
- choose portfolio surfaces where AI can improve more than one category at once
- connect every strategic AI solution back to repo IDs, product IDs, and platform IDs
- keep delivery-specific packaging separate from reusable solution logic
- use scorecards, trackers, and maturity status to pace scaling
- review portfolio AI strategy after each major rollout wave
