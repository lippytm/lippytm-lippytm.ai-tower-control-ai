# Monthly Close to Tax Workflow

## Purpose
This workflow connects monthly accounting close activities to quarterly and year-end tax-support reporting so the Business of Businesses ecosystem stays audit-ready throughout the year rather than only at filing time.

## Goals
- reduce year-end cleanup
- improve accountability and traceability
- catch exceptions earlier
- maintain documentation discipline
- improve multi-entity visibility
- make AI and dashboard support safer and more useful

## Workflow Stages
### Stage 1 — Collect and Normalize Source Data
Gather:
- bank and card activity
- bookkeeping exports
- invoices and receipts
- payroll and contractor reports
- subscription/platform charges
- CRM and revenue source data
- intercompany activity logs

Outputs:
- normalized source files
- document reference IDs
- period-specific entity folders or records

### Stage 2 — Reconcile and Review Core Accounts
Complete:
- bank reconciliations
- card reconciliations
- revenue checks
- expense categorization checks
- payroll and contractor reviews
- asset/liability review

Outputs:
- updated reconciliations
- unresolved difference list
- preliminary exception register

### Stage 3 — Update Cost-Center and Multi-Entity Views
Complete:
- cost-center assignments
- shared-cost allocations
- intercompany reconciliations
- parent/child or affiliate rollups

Outputs:
- updated cost-center schedule
- intercompany reconciliation file
- multi-entity notes

### Stage 4 — Run Internal Controls Checklist
Complete:
- documentation completeness checks
- personal/business separation review
- unusual transaction review
- prior-period follow-up
- tax-sensitive item flagging

Outputs:
- updated controls checklist
- escalation items

### Stage 5 — Produce Monthly Accountability Summary
Summarize:
- revenue and expense highlights
- open exceptions
- missing documents
- items requiring owner action
- items requiring professional review later

Outputs:
- monthly accountability report
- dashboard update
- AI-assisted draft summary under human review

### Stage 6 — Quarter / Year-End Accumulation
At quarter-end or year-end:
- compile monthly results
- review trends in exceptions
- compare against prior periods
- prepare tax-support reporting package

Outputs:
- quarter or year summary
- draft Tax Return Audit Report
- professional-review packet where needed

## Suggested Cadence
### Monthly
- collect source data
- reconcile accounts
- review expenses and revenue
- update exception register
- update document index
- update dashboard

### Quarterly
- review trends and repeat exceptions
- review contractor and payroll support
- review owner activity and intercompany patterns
- review cost-center allocations

### Year-End
- compile annual report package
- finalize open exception strategy
- prepare professional review packet
- archive final versions

## Roles
### Business Owner / Operator
- reviews major exceptions
- approves assumptions or escalation
- ensures final decisions are documented

### Internal Preparer / Reviewer
- maintains templates
- updates controls checklist
- prepares monthly and year-end reports
- tracks document completeness

### Professional Reviewer
- reviews uncertain tax-sensitive items
- validates treatment where needed
- advises on unresolved or material issues

### AI / Bot Support Layer
- drafts summaries
- requests missing documents
- flags incomplete checklist items
- updates structured logs
- routes unresolved items for human review

## Best Practices
- close every month even if lightly at first
- preserve the same reference IDs across all stages
- do not postpone exception logging to year-end
- link dashboard cards back to report sections and documents
- treat repeated small problems as potential control issues
- use AI to organize and summarize, not to replace judgment
